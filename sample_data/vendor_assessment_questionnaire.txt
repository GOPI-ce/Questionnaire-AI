NovaMed Health — Vendor Security Assessment Questionnaire
Submitted by: Midwest Regional Health System
Date: March 2025

Instructions: Please answer each question based on your current security posture and practices. Attach supporting documentation where applicable.

1. What encryption standards does your platform use for data in transit and data at rest?

2. Does your platform comply with HIPAA? Do you hold any third-party security certifications (e.g., SOC 2, HITRUST, ISO 27001)?

3. How do you handle access control for your employees and for customers accessing the system?

4. What is your process for detecting and responding to security vulnerabilities?

5. What is your incident response procedure in the event of a data breach involving PHI?

6. What are your uptime SLA commitments, and what was your actual historical uptime in 2024?

7. Where is customer data stored geographically? Is any data processed or transferred outside the United States?

8. How frequently do you perform penetration testing and security assessments?

9. What are your backup and disaster recovery capabilities? Please include RTO and RPO targets.

10. How do you manage third-party vendors and subprocessors who may have access to PHI?

11. Do you support GDPR compliance requirements for EU data subjects?

12. What logging and audit trail capabilities does your platform provide?

13. What happens to our data if we terminate our contract with you?

14. Do you sell or share patient data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes?

15. What support options are available, and what are your response time commitments?
